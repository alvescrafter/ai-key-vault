## Detailed Description

🔐 AI Key Vault — The secure way to store and manage your AI API keys.

Tired of digging through password managers or notes apps to find your API keys? AI Key Vault keeps them encrypted and ready to copy with one click.

🔒 MILITARY-GRADE ENCRYPTION
Your keys are encrypted with AES-256-GCM using a master password derived via PBKDF2 (100,000 iterations). Your master password is never stored — only you can unlock the vault.

📋 ONE-CLICK COPY
Click any key in the popup to copy it to your clipboard instantly. No more fumbling with password managers — just click and paste.

🌐 SMART SITE DETECTION
The vault widget automatically appears on supported AI sites (ChatGPT, OpenAI Platform, Anthropic Console, Google AI Studio, Groq Console, DeepSeek Platform) when you have matching keys.

⏱️ AUTO-LOCK
Vault automatically locks after 30 minutes of inactivity. Close your browser? The vault is locked. Your encrypted keys stay safe.

⌨️ KEYBOARD SHORTCUT
Press Ctrl+Shift+K (Cmd+Shift+K on Mac) on any page to toggle the vault widget.

SUPPORTED PROVIDERS:
• OpenAI (ChatGPT, Platform)
• Anthropic (Claude Console)
• Google (Gemini AI Studio)
• Groq Console
• DeepSeek Platform
• Custom providers

SECURITY NOTES:
• Master password is never stored — only a salt is persisted
• Keys are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM
• Decrypted keys only live in memory, cleared on lock
• Session data cleared on browser restart
• No data ever leaves your device
